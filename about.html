About the Club

We’re a friendly, volunteer‑run tennis club serving Seton and surrounding neighborhoods. Whether you’re picking up a racquet for the first time or playing league matches, you’ll find partners, coaches, and courts here.

We keep costs low by keeping things simple: community nights, clinics, junior development, and a monthly box ladder. As we grow, we’ll add court booking tools and more events.

History

Founded in 2025 by local players who wanted a welcoming, low‑barrier club. Our first season focused on socials and juniors; year two adds ladders and more clinics.
